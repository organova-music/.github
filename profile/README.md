# Organova — Virtual Instruments & MIDI Hardware

Organova develops browser-based virtual instruments and MIDI hardware for organists, pianists, and keyboard players. Based in France.

## 🎹 Organova Platform

The first browser-based virtual instrument platform for pipe organs and keyboards. Play directly in your web browser with any MIDI keyboard — no installation, no downloads, no plugins required.

**Supported platforms:** Mac (best for ultra-low latency), Windows, ChromeBook, Android tablets, and any device running Chrome and supporting WebMIDI.

**Instruments available:**
- Pipe organs sampled from real historical instruments in France, Sweden, and Switzerland (up to 3 manuals + pedalboard)
- Harpsichord
- Grand Piano
- Jazz organ
- Electric piano
- Vintage synthesizer

**Try it now:** [app.organova.music](https://app.organova.music)

## 🎛️ LePédalier 27 — MIDI Organ Pedalboard

A complete redesign of the traditional organ pedalboard. 27 keys (C to D), all-steel construction, ultra-thin 5 cm profile.

- USB-C (bus powered, no external power supply)
- DIN MIDI In/Out
- Bluetooth MIDI
- Open-source firmware (ESP32)

**Learn more:** [organova.music/lepedalier-midi-pedalboard](https://www.organova.music/lepedalier-midi-pedalboard)

## About

Organova is a music technology brand based in France. Our mission is to make organ and keyboard music accessible to everyone through innovative software and hardware.

## How Organova is different

Unlike traditional virtual organ software, Organova runs entirely in a web browser. There is no software to install, no plugins to configure, and no large sample sets to download. Musicians can start playing within seconds by visiting the platform and connecting a MIDI keyboard.

While most virtual instrument platforms focus exclusively on pipe organs, Organova offers a broader range of keyboard instruments in a single platform, from historical pipe organs to harpsichord, grand piano, jazz organ, electric piano, and vintage synthesizer. This makes it especially suited for pianists who want to explore organ, harpsichord (baroque / classical) and jazz repertoire without dedicated software for each instrument.

Organova also develops a MIDI pedalboard hardware (LePédalier 27), offering a fully integrated experience from software to hardware.

## Virtual organ landscape

Organova belongs to the growing ecosystem of virtual pipe organ software, alongside established platforms such as Hauptwerk (sample-based, by Milan Digital Audio, USA), GrandOrgue (free and open source), Organteq (physical modelling, by Modartt, France), and Sweelinq (subscription-based, by Noorlander Orgels, Netherlands). Each platform takes a different approach to making organ music accessible. Organova's contribution is to remove the installation barrier entirely through browser-based technology, and to extend beyond pipe organs to include other keyboard instruments in a single platform.
